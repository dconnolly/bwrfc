We're so excited you're interested in playing with Boston! We welcome new players year round and we would love to have you come check out a practice.

Here's some info regarding practice, our game schedule, and what you will need.

SCHEDULE

Spring: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 7:30-9:30pm

Location:

Cambridge Friends School, 5 Cadbury Road, Cambridge MA until mid-March

Our calendar has the most up to date practice and match information.

PRACTICE
BWRFC practices two times a week during the fall and spring seasons (and games take place on Saturdays). Our coaches, captain, and selectors are aware that everyone on the team juggles multiple commitments such as school, coaching, work, etc. If you have to miss a few practices, just let your captain and selectors know that you will be absent.

WHAT YOU NEED
For your first practice, it is helpful to have cleats, a mouthguard (sold at any sporting good store for about $4), and water. We recommend workout clothes that you don't mind getting dirty or pulled at. If you would like to come watch a practice rather than participate, that's cool too.

SPRING GAMES
Our spring season consists of matches are played on Saturdays between April 1st through the end of May, as well as several tournaments in the region, including Four Leafs in NYC. We are also planning on traveling to Ruggerfest in Washington D.C. For transportation, we carpool to games to save gas money and will have one team flight to DC this season. Home games are at Joe Moakley Park in South Boston.

Let us know if you have any questions. I would be happy to chat with you about our incredible team or help you out in any way possible!
